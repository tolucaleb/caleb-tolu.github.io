Last updated: July 7, 2025

Axmof Omni ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of your store data and your customers' information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect the data provided through our Shopify app.

We collect and store the following types of information when you install or use our app:

From Merchants:

From Store Customers:

We use the data we collect to:

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties except:

We take data security seriously. We store your information securely using encrypted systems and limit access to authorized personnel only.

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide you with the app's services.
